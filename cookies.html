Cookies Policy

At Zenko Books, we value your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how we use cookies on our website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help improve your browsing experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our site.

2. How We Use Cookies
  • Essential Cookies: Required for the basic operation of our website, such as secure login and checkout functions.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site so we can improve functionality and user experience.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings, such as language and region, for a more personalized experience.
  • Analytics Cookies: Used to analyze traffic and visitor behavior to enhance our services.
3. Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use trusted third-party services for analytics or payment processing. These providers may place cookies to deliver their services securely and efficiently.

5. Updates to This Policy

Zenko Books may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
