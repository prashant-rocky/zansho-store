Refund, Return & Shipping Policy

Your satisfaction is our priority. Please read our policies carefully.

Refund & Return Policy

We at Zansho Store strive to ensure that you are completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not happy with your order, you may be eligible for a refund or exchange under the following conditions:

  • Products must be returned within 14 days of delivery.
  • Items must be unused, in original packaging, and in the same condition as received.
  • To request a return, please contact our support team at ict_ebook@kinokuniya.co.jp with your order details.
  • Refunds will be processed to your original payment method within 7-10 business days after receiving the returned product.
  • Shipping charges for returned items are the responsibility of the customer, unless the product is defective or damaged.

Exchanges are subject to product availability. We reserve the right to refuse returns that do not meet the above conditions.

Shipping Policy

We aim to deliver your orders as quickly and efficiently as possible:

  • Orders are processed within 1-2 business days after payment confirmation.
  • Shipping times may vary depending on your location:
    • Domestic (Japan): 2-5 business days
    • International: 7-21 business days
  • Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and order size.
  • You will receive a tracking number via email once your order has been dispatched.
  • Please ensure your shipping address is correct to avoid delays.

For any questions regarding shipping, returns, or refunds, please contact our support team at ict_ebook@kinokuniya.co.jp.
